Withit wardrobe is a luxury British streetwear brand creating high-quality garmetns in Manchester, UK. Looking to collaborate high-end fashion with purpose. All designs are creafted in England and handmade in Europe. The 'Withit.' vision is simple: to build a fashion brand empowering the people around us.

Withit was founded by Faisal Tayan and was built on a foundation of empathy. From a background in industrial design, it gave Faisal a different perspective on what a brand should be. This was met with his eagerness to help and with these two principles, this is where Withit was cultivated.
